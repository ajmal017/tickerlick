TICKERLICK

TICKERLICK is technical signal generator system to assist traders to find buy/sell signals for stocks traded in NYSE, NASDAQ and AMEX exchanges. The signals are generated by using live and historical price data. We use MACD (moving average convergence/divergence) (12,26,9), MACD (5,35,5), RSI, Stochastic and Bollinger Band technical analysis indicators to generate signals.

Tickerlick is based in Sunnyvale, California.
